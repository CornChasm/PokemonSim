Size Calibration

Adjust the window size/zoom on the next page until it fits both the button on the right side and the box on the left side

Party Box

The party box is on the left side of the screen, it shows your chosen pokemon, during the battle phase, the enemy party box will be shown on the right side of the screen

Game/Center Box

This is where choosing pokemon, choosing moves and battling takes place, it was designed to resemble old pokemon games so it has a set size across all pages

Choosing Pokemon

You choose pokemon by clicking on them and clicking "Add To Party", after choosing 6 pokemon you go back to the first party slot, where you can change your choices

Choosing Moves

You choose moves by first clicking on a party pokemon, then on a move you want to add and clicking "Add to Moveset", it cycles the same way as choosing pokemon

Pokemon Types

Pokemon can have (in this simulator) 17 different types, they can either be Super Effective(2x), Not Very Effective(0.5x) or Immune(0x) against each other

Move Types

Moves can have the same types as pokemon, their effectiveness against a pokemon is shown during battle

Move Categories

Moves are divided into 3 categories
Physical - ATK and DEF
Special - SPATK and SPDEF
Status - give effects to pokemon (ATK UP, DEF DOWN, BURN etc.)

STAB moves

Moves that have the same type as the pokemon get an additional 0.5x increase in ATK/SPATK
